The Team



Dr. Robert Pless

Dr. Robert Pless conducts research in the area of computer vision with applications to environmental science, medical imaging, robotics and virtual reality. At a high level, the current themes of research in his lab are: understanding visual change at scales from the sidewalk to the planet, studying next generation imaging systems for virtual reality and robotics, and democratizing visual analytics with applications to social justice.
