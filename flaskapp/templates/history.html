Background

All About AMOS

The Archive of Many Outdoor Scenes (AMOS) dataset contains images taken from webcams located around the world, the vast majority in the United States. Construction of AMOS began in March, 2006 and continues to this day. This database is unique because it contains many natural outdoor scenes in consecutive and continous periods.

Webcams

The webcams have been selected by undergraduate and graduate students. Additional cameras have been added as we find interesting and unique views. If you would like to add a camera to the database, please visit this link to submit the url and name of the camera!

Why collect so many images

Collecting and storing all of this data is useful for studing changes in climate, environment, temperature, and behavior of objects in the view of these webcams. The ideas of analysis on this data is limitless and continuing to this day.
