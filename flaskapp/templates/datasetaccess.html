Using the Dataset

The dataset's more than 1 billion images of natural outdoor environments can be used to study changes in environment, climate, and
temperature. AMOS can also be used to study variations in the behavior of people, animals, and objects. Previously,
AMOS has been used for studies in webcam geolocation, geo-orientation, and scene annotation.
